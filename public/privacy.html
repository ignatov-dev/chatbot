Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

1. What We Collect

When you create an account we store your email address and an authentication token. When you use the chatbot we store your conversation messages to provide conversation history.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate the chatbot service — authenticating you, saving your conversations, and retrieving relevant answers. We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Supabase for authentication and database hosting, and Groq for language model inference. Your messages are sent to these providers to deliver the service. Google OAuth is available as an optional sign-in method.

4. Data Storage & Security

Data is stored in Supabase-managed PostgreSQL databases with row-level security enabled. All traffic is encrypted via TLS. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text.

5. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon deletion, all conversations and personal data are permanently removed.

6. Cookies

We use only essential cookies required for authentication. No tracking or analytics cookies are used.

7. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, reach us at support@xbo.com.
